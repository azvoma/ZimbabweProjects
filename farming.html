Fund farming in Zimbabwe
milestone by milestone

Release capital for land prep, seeds, irrigation, and harvest only when your farmer uploads GPS-verified photos at each stage.

Land preparation
Fund ploughing and soil preparation as milestone 1. Approve only when photos show tilled land with GPS coordinates matching your farm's registered location and a timestamp.
Seed & inputs purchase
Release the seed & fertiliser budget only when the farmer uploads supplier invoices and a photo of the purchased stock at the farm. Cross-check receipts against the amount funded.
Harvest & delivery
The final milestone releases when your farmer uploads harvest photos and a delivery weight ticket from the GMB depot or buyer — confirming the crop made it to market.
