Fund medical care in Zimbabwe
with verified receipts

Cover hospital admissions, surgery, medication and ongoing treatment — every payment verified by official medical documents.

Healthcare funding with ZimTrust moves quickly. For urgent hospital admissions we support same-day approval for funded milestones — contact us at urgent@zimtrust.co.uk once your project is created.

Hospital admission
Fund the deposit and initial consultation. Release is triggered by the hospital admission letter and deposit receipt — typically available within hours of admission.
Surgery & procedure
Release the surgical payment when the booking confirmation and pre-op letter are uploaded. Remaining balance releases on the discharge summary from the hospital.
Ongoing medication
Set monthly milestones for medication costs, each releasing when the pharmacy receipt for that month is uploaded — maintaining continuity of care without overpaying.
