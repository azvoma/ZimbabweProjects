Our story

Why we built ZimTrust

In 2022, our founder Chiedza Mutasa sent £18,000 to a contractor in Harare to build a family home. The contractor disappeared after laying the foundation. The money was gone. There was no recourse.

That experience — shared by thousands of diaspora families every year — led to ZimTrust. We spent two years talking to families in Birmingham, Manchester, and London, and to contractors, farmers, and school administrators in Zimbabwe, to understand exactly what a trustworthy system would look like.
