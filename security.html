Your money is protected
at every step

We built ZimTrust on a foundation of regulatory compliance, financial segregation, and cryptographic data integrity.

FCA Authorised
ZimTrust Ltd is authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority as an Authorised Payment Institution (Reference 987654). Regulated under the Payment Services Regulations 2017.
Segregated client money
All escrow funds are held in dedicated client money accounts, completely separate from ZimTrust operating funds. Protected up to £85,000 under FSCS.
End-to-end encryption
All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3. Evidence files (photos, videos) are stored encrypted at rest using AES-256. Keys are managed with hardware security modules.
Immutable audit logs
Every action is appended to an append-only audit log with cryptographic hashing. Records cannot be altered or deleted, providing a tamper-evident trail for disputes.
