Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2025

1. Who we are

ZimTrust Ltd (“ZimTrust”, “we”, “us”) is a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. 15847392), authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority as an Authorised Payment Institution (Reference 987654). Our registered office is at 20 Finsbury Street, London, EC2Y 9AQ.

2. What data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity data: full name, date of birth, nationality
  • Contact data: email address, phone number, postal address
  • Financial data: bank account details, EcoCash number, transaction history
  • Identity verification: passport or national ID scans (for KYC compliance)
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, device and browser information
  • Evidence files: photos and videos uploaded as project proof-of-work

3. How we use your data

We use your data to provide the ZimTrust escrow service, process payments, comply with FCA and anti-money laundering regulations, communicate service updates and notifications, and improve our platform. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data retention

Financial records are retained for 7 years as required by HMRC and FCA regulations. Identity verification data is retained for 5 years after account closure. You may request deletion of non-regulatory data at any time by emailing privacy@zimtrust.co.uk.

5. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, erase, restrict, and port your data. You also have the right to object to processing and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@zimtrust.co.uk.

6. Contact

Our Data Protection Officer can be reached at privacy@zimtrust.co.uk or at our registered office address above.
