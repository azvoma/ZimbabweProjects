How release fees work

A release fee is charged only when you approve a milestone and funds are disbursed. On the Starter plan, a 2.5% fee applies to the released amount. On Pro, the fee is 1.5%. If a milestone is cancelled before proof is submitted, no release fee applies — you receive the full escrow back minus our 0.5% cancellation fee.

Example: You fund a £2,000 roofing milestone on the Pro plan. When you approve it, £30 (1.5%) is deducted from the escrow and £1,970 is sent to the recipient. No other fees apply.
