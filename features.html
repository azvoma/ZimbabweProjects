Platform features

Built for trust.
Designed for accountability.

Every feature in ZimTrust exists to answer one question: did the work happen?

Milestone escrow board
Break any project into funded milestones on a kanban-style board. Each column — Pending, In Progress, Needs Review, Paid — maps to a real stage in your project. Money only advances when stages are verified.
GPS-verified proof of work
Every photo and video upload is tagged with device GPS coordinates and an upload timestamp. Images are cross-checked against the project's registered site location so they cannot be faked from another location.
FCA-regulated escrow wallet
Funds are held in a dedicated client money account regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority. Your money is never commingled with ZimTrust operating funds and is protected up to £85,000 under FSCS.
Project timeline view
Visualise every milestone, due date, and dependency in a timeline view. Both sender and recipient see the same real-time view — no information asymmetry, no excuses.
Real-time notifications
Instant alerts via in-app push, email, and SMS (WhatsApp Business integration coming Q3 2025). Know the moment proof is uploaded, a milestone is approved, or funds are disbursed.
Immutable audit trail
Every action is appended to a tamper-evident log — who did what, when, with which files. Exportable as PDF for disputes, legal proceedings, or your own records.
EcoCash & bank payouts
Approve a milestone and funds arrive in the recipient's EcoCash wallet, Steward Bank, ZB Bank, or CBZ account within 48 hours. No trips to Western Union. No cut-out middlemen.
Dispute resolution
Raise a dispute flag on any milestone. Our mediation team reviews the evidence trail and facilitates a fair outcome within 5 business days — faster and cheaper than any legal action.
Multi-sender pooling
Family or community groups can pool contributions from multiple senders into a single project escrow. Each sender sees their contribution and the overall project progress on a shared dashboard.
