1. Acceptance

By creating an account or using ZimTrust, you agree to these Terms of Service and our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you must not use the platform.

2. The ZimTrust service

ZimTrust provides a milestone-based escrow platform that holds funds on behalf of senders (“Senders”) and disburses those funds to recipients (“Recipients”) in Zimbabwe upon the Sender’s approval of milestone proof submissions. ZimTrust is not a bank and does not provide investment advice.

3. Sender obligations

Senders agree to: (a) fund milestones in advance before work commences; (b) review proof submissions in good faith within 14 days of submission; (c) not use ZimTrust to fund illegal activities; (d) complete identity verification as required by FCA AML regulations.

4. Recipient obligations

Recipients agree to: (a) upload honest, unedited evidence of work completed; (b) not submit fraudulent GPS-tagged photos; (c) complete identity and payout account verification; (d) accept that ZimTrust may withhold payouts pending dispute resolution.

5. Fees

Platform fees are charged as a percentage of each milestone release (2.5% on Starter, 1.5% on Pro). A 0.5% cancellation fee applies to funded milestones cancelled before proof submission. All fees are deducted from the escrow at the point of disbursement and confirmed in advance.

6. Disputes

Either party may raise a dispute within 14 days of a milestone decision. ZimTrust’s mediation team will issue a determination within 5 business days. ZimTrust’s determination is binding. You retain the right to pursue legal remedies independently.

7. Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.
