Fund house construction in Zimbabwe
with photographic accountability

Release payments for each building stage only when your recipient uploads GPS-verified photos — foundation, walls, roof, and finishes.

Why milestone escrow

Stop funding disappearing contractors

Before ZimTrust, our founder sent £18,000 to a contractor who vanished after laying the foundation. ZimTrust was built so this never happens to another diaspora family. By splitting your budget into milestones and locking funds in escrow, no single stage can be overpaid or skipped without photographic proof.

  • Contractor paid only after each stage is photographed
  • GPS coordinates verified against your registered plot
  • Full audit trail exportable as PDF at any time
  • Dispute resolution if contractor disagrees
Typical construction milestones

Foundation milestone
Lock 20% of your budget for groundwork. Release only when photos show foundation poured to DPC level with your project's GPS tag — taken at the registered plot coordinates.
Walls & brickwork
Approve payment for walls once your contractor uploads photos of brickwork at window-sill and wall-plate height — each as a separate milestone, each GPS-tagged to your stand.
Roof & finishes
Fund roofing, plastering, tiling, and electrical as separate milestones — no stage can be skipped or overpaid without your explicit approval and photographic evidence.
