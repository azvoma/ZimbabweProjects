Fund borehole drilling in Zimbabwe
depth by depth

Release drilling payments only when GPS-verified photos show verified depth, casing, and pump installation at your registered site.

Site survey & rig mobilisation
Release the hydro-geological survey fee when the report is uploaded, and the rig mobilisation fee only when a GPS photo shows the drilling rig on your registered land.
Drilling progress
Stage drilling payments in 20-metre increments. Each tranche releases when a photo shows the depth marker and GPS coordinates match your registered plot — preventing billing for undrilled metres.
Pump & commissioning
The final milestone releases when the installed pump photo, pressure test results, and a measured flow rate reading are all uploaded — confirming a working water source.
