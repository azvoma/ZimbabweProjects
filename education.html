Fund education in Zimbabwe
term by term, receipt by receipt

Send school fees knowing every payment is verified by official receipts and enrolment confirmation before being released to your recipient.

Term-by-term release
Structure the full year's fees as three milestones — one per term. Each releases only when the official school receipt for that term is uploaded and verified.
Enrolment verification
Require an official enrolment letter before Term 1 fees are released — confirming the child is registered at the school of your choosing at the agreed level.
Report card milestone
Add an optional end-of-term milestone: the next term's fees only release once a signed report card is uploaded — aligning your funding with academic progress.
